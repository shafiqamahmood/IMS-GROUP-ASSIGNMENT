About Product

Our cakes are proudly Halal certified to the highest standard from the Department of Islamic Development of Malaysia (JAKIM).

All cakes must be kept refrigerated (2°c – 6°c) at all times before serving.

All cakes are made using fresh ingredients, we strongly recommend to consume the cake within 24 hours from the time of delivery. If there’s any leftovers, please keep the cake fully sealed in an air-tight plastic container in the fridge to prevent dehydration/contamination and consume as soon as possible.
